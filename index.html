About Us

Crescente fully automates and centralizes risk management for banking the cannabis industry. The system provides data monitoring, recordkeeping, and reporting to eliminate the time-consuming and error-prone manual process and mitigates the risks associated with banking the cannabis industry. Batch transaction data and near-real-time customer data can be imported from core processing platforms, wire transfer and teller systems, and state grower databases and stored in a centralized database for ongoing access and analysis. Crescente automates the customer due diligence required during the account opening process, assigns risk ratings based on responses to specific questions, and supports the accurate evaluation of risk based on proven metrics. The capabilities to profile existing customers, perform peer group analyses, maintain research analyst notes and actions, and generate management, board, and auditor reports are also provided.

It Works Like This

Crescente is an enterprise application for monitoring and reporting suspicious financial activities in the cannabis industry. This solution automates any manual process, saving banks valuable time and resources while reducing risks.

Crescente uses browser-based technology to facilitate ease-of-use. It “Knows Your Customer:

  • Crescente enables banks to gather customer due diligence and expected account volume information during the account opening process. Banks can define questions that gather metrics for evaluating risks, and each customer is then assigned a risk rating. Profiling for existing customers can also be conducted.
  • Crescente also performs peer group analyses, which can help eliminate the complicated manual monitoring and linking of independent accounts to a group.
  • Crescente enables research analysts’ actions and notes to be made and stored for easy reference. As accounts are reviewed, a dashboard displays the analyses for the overriding officer to approve.
  • Crescente also stores analysts’ work in order to produce manager, board, and auditor reports for any defined time period. This process allows for easy and accurate recordkeeping and meets data storage requirements.
  • Crescente imports suspicious activities into its case management module for extended tracking and follow-up. Cases can be annotated with notes as well as any type of attached document or image file.
